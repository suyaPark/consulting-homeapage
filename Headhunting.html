HEADHUNTING



Commencing business operations in Vietnam presents a common obstacle for foreign entrepreneurs who encounter difficulties in locating appropriate local manpower to assist them in their new business venture. Nonetheless, there exists a pool of highly skilled and capable local individuals who actively seek job prospects. Paradoxically, businesses often face challenges in identifying qualified candidates, while job seekers encounter multiple hurdles in their quest for suitable positions.

Our aim is to address the inefficiencies arising from the mismatch between foreign entrepreneurs and local labor by offering our specialized services. We strive to facilitate the discovery of skilled local employees for foreign entrepreneurs while providing promising young individuals with opportunities to work alongside foreign-invested enterprises. In this endeavor, Park & Duong Consulting acts as an intermediary connecting foreign-invested enterprises with both aspiring job seekers and seasoned professionals.
