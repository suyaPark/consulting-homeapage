VISA

Based on the purpose of entry into Vietnam, Vietnam visas are classified into 21 different main categories. Among them, the six most popular visa types are:

✓ Tourist Visa (DL)
✓ Business Visa (DN1 – DN2)
✓ Work Visa (LD1 – LD2)
✓ Investment Visa (DT1, DT2, DT3, DT4)
✓ Visa to visit relatives (TT)
✓ Electronic Visa (EV)

Types of Vietnam visas Level object Corresponding term
DT Granted to foreign investors or representatives of foreign organizations investing in Vietnam, corresponding to each capital level, specifically:
(i) DT1: from VND 100 billion or more;
(ii) DT2: from 50 billion to less than 100 billion;
(iii) DT3: from 03 billion to less than 50 billion VND;
(iv) DT4: less than 03 billion.		 DT1, DT2:Term not exceeding 5 years
DT3: The term does not exceed 3 years
DT4: The term does not exceed 12 months
DN DN1 - Issued to foreigners working with other enterprises and organizations with legal status in accordance with Vietnamese law.
DN2 - Issued to foreigners to offer services, establish commercial presence, and perform other activities under international treaties to which Vietnam is a signatory.		 No more than 12 months
LD Issued to people entering the workforce:
LD1 - Issued to foreigners working in Vietnam with confirmation that they are not eligible for a work permit
LD2 - Issued to foreigners working in Vietnam who are required to have a work permit.		 No more than 2 years
DL Issued to people entering tourism No more than 3 months
THIS E-visa - issued to foreigners applying for a short-term visa to Vietnam. No more than 30 days

Vietnam visa has many types, each type is issued for a different entry purpose. When the visa expires, depending on the case, you can carry out the extension procedure to be able to continue to stay legally in Vietnam.
Each type of visa requires different application doccuments and qualifications. For more information on each type of visa, contact us for advice and support.
