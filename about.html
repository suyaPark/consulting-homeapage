About P&D consulting

Our Story

Since opening our first global office in 2020, we have encountered many challenges and difficulties in our business. We have seen other companies expanding overseas face similar difficulties and struggles for the first time, so we wanted to help them and started our current consulting service.
We provide global-level business consulting services to local and international entrepreneurs. We provide customised solutions consultations with our clients and will do our best to ensure that your business runs smoothly, with customer satisfaction as our top priority.

P&D Consulting is a consulting & IT software firm that provides company formation, management, accounting closing/audit and tax advisory services.

P&D Consulting is a global business consultancy specialising in providing coordination, advisory and administrative services to support a wide range of domestic and foreign small and medium-sized ventures and start-ups, promising to provide your company with optimal professional services at reasonable prices, with advantages such as efficient government regulations, low tax burden, advanced government systems and logistics.
