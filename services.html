Our Services

Vietnam, a country that has historically been characterized by its self-reliant approach and protected market, has gradually opened its doors to foreign investment following significant economic reforms in the late 20th century. During the period of rapid growth and development, the government implemented measures to foster domestic industries by limiting foreign influence and competition. However, these experiences taught Vietnam valuable lessons, particularly during times of crisis, emphasizing the undeniable force of globalization and the need to embrace it.

Recognizing the importance of opening up to the global market, Vietnam introduced the Foreign Investment Law, providing a legal framework in 1987 to attract and facilitate foreign investment. This marked a significant shift in Vietnam's approach, transforming it into a country that enthusiastically welcomes foreign investors for the betterment of its own economic landscape.

Nevertheless, despite the government's proactive stance in welcoming foreign businesses, navigating the Vietnamese market can still present challenges for international entrepreneurs due to language barriers, unfamiliar legal and cultural practices, and distinct business and living environments. Park & Duong Consulting recognizes these obstacles and offers a comprehensive range of services tailored to address them, effectively minimizing barriers and facilitating successful entry into the Vietnamese business landscape.

At Park & Duong Consulting, we understand that each business endeavor is unique, and as such, we provide customized solutions designed to optimize opportunities for our clients. Our team of seasoned professionals possesses extensive knowledge and experience in Vietnam's business landscape, enabling us to offer invaluable insights, expert guidance, and personalized support.

Whether you are considering establishing a new venture or expanding your existing business in Vietnam, Park & Duong Consulting is committed to providing you with the necessary tools and assistance to navigate the complexities of the Vietnamese market. We offer a diverse range of services that cater to the specific needs of foreign entrepreneurs, ensuring a smooth and successful business transition.

Partner with Park & Duong Consulting today, and let us help you unlock the immense potential of Vietnam's dynamic and rapidly growing economy. Together, we can embark on a rewarding journey towards business success and mutual prosperity.
