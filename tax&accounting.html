TAXATION & ACCOUNTING

The way a business thrives in a foreign nation is often linked to its approach towards the tax policies established by the local government. In Vietnam, the government offers specific tax advantages to attract investments and foster business growth. As an illustration, foreign individuals with professional roles have the option to opt for a fixed tax rate for their personal income. Additionally, foreign investors who meet the requirements for certain business categories and/or meet the minimum investment threshold can benefit from substantial tax incentives within Free Economic Zones (FEZ), Free Investment Zones (FIZ), and Free Trade special industrial complexes.
